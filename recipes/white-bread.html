White Bread Recipe

This white bread recipe is the best at home recipe for soft, fluffy, buttery sandwich bread at home.

If you make bread regularly at home, you should already have all the ingredients you need for this uncomplicated recipe, although I highly suggest getting a Stand Mixer if you dont already have one, to make your life easier

Ingredients

  • Flour 440 g
  • Sugar 21 g
  • Salt 8 g
  • Butter 3 TBSP
  • Yeast 9 g
  • Water 174 g
  • Milk 125 g

Steps

  1. Mix water, milk, and yeast and let bloom (ready to use when the top has a "frothy" layer.
  2. Add dry ingredients in a separate bowl or to stand mixer bowl and mix together. Once fully mixed, add your water and yeast mixture.
  3. Mix with dough hook until it forms a smooth ball (about 4-5 minutes, scrape sides along the way).
  4. Slowly add butter, about 1 TBSP at a time, while mixing.
  5. Knead for 7-10 minutes or until it passsd the window pane test.
  6. Once it's done kneading, cover with damp towel or oiled plastic wrap ad let proof about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  7. Ater it has proofed, roll the mixture into a flat rectangle the same width as the bread pan, and starting from the bottom roll into a log and set into the greased bread pan.
  8. Let the roll rise one hour or until doubled in size.
  9. preheat oven to 350ยบ.
  10. Insert pan into the hot oven and let bake for 40-45 minutes or until brown on top and the bread makes a hollow sound when tapped on.
  11. Once the bread is done, turn it out of the pan onto a cooling rack. You are able to cut into it once it has cooled completely.
